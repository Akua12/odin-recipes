Egg-in-a-Hole

Description

A delicious runny yolk egg cooked in the center of a piece of toast and eaten with a fork is a great way to enjoy an egg in a slightly different way. My kids feel fancy when eating these.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons bacon grease
  • 1 sliced bread
  • 1 egg
  • Salt &Ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt the bacon grease in a non-stick pan over low heat.
  2. Cut a 1 1/2 to 2-inch hole from the center of the bread slice; lay in the hot skillet.
  3. When the side facing down is lightly toasted, about 2 minutes, flip and crack the egg into the hole; season with salt and pepper.
  4. ontinue to cook until the egg is cooked and mostly firm.
  5. Flip again and cook 1 minute more to assure doneness on both sides.
